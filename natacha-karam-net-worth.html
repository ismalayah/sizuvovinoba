Natacha Karam is an actress. She recently started her career in 2017, debuting in the TV series Silent Witness. That same year, she guest-starred in an episode of Homeland, as well as appeared in the TV film Valentine’s Again. Her first starring role on television was as Jasmine ‘Jaz’ Khan in The Brave, which she played from 2017 to 2018. After this, Karam made her film debut in The Hurricane Heist. Later, she guest-starred in an episode of Casualty. She will appear in the upcoming movie The Old Guard. It was also announced that she’ll join the main cast of the upcoming show 9-1-1: Lone Star.
