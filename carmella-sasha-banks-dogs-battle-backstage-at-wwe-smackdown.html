Carmella &amp; Sasha Banks Dogs Battle Backstage At WWE SmackDown

September 19, 2024
blog

There was plenty of action in the ring during SmackDown last night, but there was also a lot going on backstage.

Sasha Banks’ corgi Ryu finally got to meet another famous pro wrestling pooch last night. Carmella brought along her new puppy Pancake to Buffalo. Of course, Corey Graves couldn’t be around last night and Carmella said that Pancake missed his daddy.

What happened next is what Kelley described as: “Ryu vs Pancake.” We’re not sure who won, but at least it looks like they had a good time getting to know each other.

It’s great that WWE Superstars work in an environment where they can bring their dogs to the job. That is not a luxury that many people can enjoy. It’s one of the many perks of being a WWE Superstar because they can make their dogs famous in the process.
