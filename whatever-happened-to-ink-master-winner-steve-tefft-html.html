Whatever Happened To Ink Master Winner Steve Tefft?

August 15, 2024
blog
Spread the love             

In 2012, the reality show “Ink Master” burst onto TV screens, offering tattoo artists a chance to showcase their skills and win big. Among the winners was Steve Tefft, a Connecticut-based artist whose love for tattoos began at the tender age of The inspiration? A tattoo featuring the grim reaper that captivated his young imagination. “Even in kindergarten, I was drawing monsters during recess,” Steve recalls with a smile.

A Self-Taught Artist’s Journey

Tefft’s journey into the world of tattoo artistry began in 1990 when he purchased his own equipment without any formal education. Starting by practicing on friends, he soon realized the importance of gaining knowledge and skills. Working at a tattoo shop became his training ground, leading him to audition for “Ink Master.” Little did he know what he was getting into, admitting, “I had no idea what I was in for.”

 Life After Winning “Ink Master”

After securing the title of Ink Master in Season 2, Tefft’s life took a significant turn. Before the show, he managed a modest tattoo shop, 12 Tattoos, in a small Connecticut town. Winning not only boosted his business but also enhanced his artistic skills. The $100,000 prize wasn’t just a windfall; it was an investment in expanding his tattoo venture.

 Business Growth and Artistic Evolution

Tefft invested his winnings wisely, turning 12 Tattoos into the go-to body piercing and tattoo studio in Southeastern Connecticut. Reflecting on his post-Ink Master journey, he shared, “I put a lot of thought and care into every tattoo now, knowing it will be judged by the world very closely. It’s really important.” The experience propelled him toward his goal of becoming “one of the great tattoo artists of the world.”

A Global Canvas on Social Media

In today’s digital age, Tefft has embraced platforms like Instagram to share his creations with a global audience. His unique style, characterized by monochromatic horror tattoos adorned with intricate details, has garnered international acclaim. Beyond the small town where 12 Tattoos began, Steve Tefft’s work now resonates on a much grander scale.

 Staying True to Roots

Despite his international success, Tefft has remained true to his roots. The monsters with bloody eyes that he drew in kindergarten still find a place in his portfolio. His signature style, rooted in a love for horror themes, has become synonymous with his name, making him a distinguished figure in the tattoo world.

The Legacy Continues

As Tefft continues to leave his mark on the global tattoo scene, his journey from a small Connecticut town to international fame serves as an inspiration. His story illustrates that passion, dedication, and a unique vision can propel an artist to extraordinary heights, even after the cameras of reality TV have long moved on.

My Final Opinion

, Steve Tefft’s life after “Ink Master” is a testament to the enduring power of artistic passion and the impact of recognition on a global stage. From a small town artist to an international sensation, Tefft’s journey is etched in ink, leaving an indelible mark on the canvas of the tattoo world.
