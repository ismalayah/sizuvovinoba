RHOBH star Erika Jayne is on the hunt for a new man, but her special someone needs to have a special something about him.

We’re unpacking the very specific conditions that the TV personality has in place for a new S/O now that she’s back in the dating game.

Who is Erika Jayne dating?

The reality TV star recently simply said: “I’m dating myself.”

However, it looks as though Erika is back in the dating pool again following her public divorce from estranged husband Tom Girardi.

According to TMZ, the reality star is, indeed, ready to mingle again. The same outlet reports that Jayne has been “on a handful of dates with different men.”

As per Page Six, Erika has opted away from dating apps and, instead, is having her friends set her up.

To the public’s knowledge, it doesn’t seem as though any of these dates have blossomed further – but who knows?

What is the reality star looking for in a new man?

We know that Erika is back in the dating game following 21 years of marriage, but what exactly is the 50-year-old on the hunt for?

In talks with TMZ, the TV personality explained that she’s looking for someone who can splash the cash. Jayne shared: “I think every girl, you know, likes a guy with money.”

The RHOBH star also added that intelligence is a pretty vital quality for a man to have, too. As for age differences, Erika disclosed that it wasn’t too much of a deciding factor – though she’d never date someone younger than 25.

Erika has a pretty set line of conditions for a new man, and she’s telling potential suitors to “slide into her DM’s” to “find out” more about her dating life, Page Six reports.

When did Erika file for divorce?

Notably, Erika Jayne filed for divorce from 82-year-old Girardi in November 2020.

The pair had been married for 21 years, but now says that “life is good. Everything is turning a positive direction.”

Here’s to hoping that Erika finds that special someone.
