In response to the situation, the Santa Monica Police Department promptly took action by evacuating Pacific Park and securing the entirety of the pier. They also mobilized the Crisis Negotiation Team to initiate contact with the individual in distress. Despite the challenges posed by the persistent noise and chaos, law enforcement successfully maintained a secure perimeter around the pier and collaborated closely with neighboring law enforcement agencies.
