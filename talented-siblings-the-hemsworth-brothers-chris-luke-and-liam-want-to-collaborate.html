Talented siblings: the Hemsworth brothers Chris, Luke and Liam want to collaborate

May 04, 2024
blog

The Hemsworth brothers Chris, Luke and Liam want to collaborate on a project one day. Photo / Getty

Chris and Liam Hemsworth one day hope to make a movie with their older brother Luke, 34.

They had not come across anything to collaborate on yet, but the talented actor siblings are certainly keen to share a screen.

"We have talked about it for a long time. I don't know what the movie will be, but possibly we will only get one chance to do it. We are waiting for the perfect thing," Liam said in an interview with The Independent newspaper.

While the idea was pleasant enough, he did wonder how the three of them would get along when working so closely together.

"When I was 17, I did one episode of a show with my eldest brother Luke. It was a kids' fantasy TV series, and it was the hardest day on set I've ever had in my life, because if he wasn't on camera, he would be doing something that was just trying to ruin me while I was on camera. And so I think it would be so difficult to make a film with my
brother."

Liam also revealed brother Chris paid off their parents' debts when he became a successful actor.

The 32-year-old star made the generous gesture before calling his father and telling him to check his bank account, Chris' younger brother Liam, 25, revealed.
