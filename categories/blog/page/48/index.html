From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia. Santa Claus (Japanese: サンタクロース Santa Claus) is a recurring character in the Pokémon anime. He is based on the Santa Claus of Christmas holiday fame. He has the same tasks as his real-world counterpart: giving gifts to good children all over the world while traveling on his sleigh. He also lives at the North Pole. In the anime Santa Claus first appeared in Holiday Hi-Jynx.
